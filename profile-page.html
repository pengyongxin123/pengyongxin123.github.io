Volume, Speed Data and Analysis

We did those two project as a team with Yunlong Zhang. By using time calculator, LIDAR gun and GPS, we collect traffic arrival time, gap acceptance, speed, travel time, delay time, then after analysis, we have time and headway distribution, speed distribution, intersection delay and level of service. Finally, we build traffic stream model to predict the future data.

swap_horiz

Performance Evaluate and Re-Design

This wonderful project is done as a team with Gene Hawkins, we used Synchro to evaluate the current performace of the operation at George Bush Dr, Harvey Mitchell Pkwy and Luther St, Harvey Mitchell Pkwy, which approve those two intersections need to be re-desined for the next 20 years. Then, we used model to predict the volume after 20 years and designed those intersections by remaining the current design, adding one through lane for the main road, changing to Super Street with no left turn and with left turn. All the operations are revised for the new designs. Finally, we chose Super Street as the best update for those two intersections according to level of service analysis result.
